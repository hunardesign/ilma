About Us
Founded on September 28, 1998, Indian Laminate Manufacturer's Association (ILMA) is a registered non-trading corporation having the registered office in Ahmedabad and having the presence across various regions of India. It is the only registered association for laminate manufacturers in India having more than 115+ members. These members are manufacturers of decorative laminates, compact laminates or high-pressure laminates. The association's membership base also comprises of few Pre-Lam, Particle Boards and Medium Density Fibre Board (MDF) Manufacturers.
