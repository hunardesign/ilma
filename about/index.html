Our Mission
Join Hands
We want the manufacturers of the laminate industry join hands for common issues and organize them through a common platform.
Stay Updated
Keep the members up-to-date on the latest developments, condition of the market and policies of the government affecting the industry.
Our Broad Objectives
Important Advices
Playing an advisory role and providing impartial opinions to resolve problems and disputes
Advocacy to Authority
Promoting the interests of the industry to the government bodies / authorities
Technical Advocacy
Providing advocacy for all sorts of technical matters
Prevent Unfair Competition
Promoting their general, material, commercial and business interests, in particular by seeking to prevent unfair competition.
Advice on Import/Export
Providing information and advice on other issues like import/exportof material to the members.
Research & Development
Promoting the mutual interests of its members in respect of research, development and standards.
Provide Manpower
Make available skilled/trained manpower to the members.
