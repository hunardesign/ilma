ILMA | Indian Laminate Manufacturer's Association
Why Become A Member?
The Association's membership has numerous directly and indirectly advantages and at ILMA, we don't consider the Membership fees as a fee rather an Investment made by the Member company.
The Association is governed by one of the best intellectuals of the Laminate Industry across the world and they bring out the best on the table to promote the Indian Laminate Industry on a global scale.
The Association's membership provides the common development for the growth of the Industry along with providing new and improved means and techniques of the productions and brand enhancement from time to time.
The Association forms various subcommittees from time to time discuss and address the areas like taxation, custom/ excise regulations, membership, technology, innovation, arbitration and much more.
These committees are comprised of Industry's Leaders/ Veterans and Young Minds. The Association hosts a number of national meetings across India at regular intervals to provide the common platform for the introduction of new technology, ideas or discuss the matters fruitful to the betterment of the Industry.
These meetings are usually hosted by the Industry leaders along with expert speakers of the particular technology. The national meetings offer a unique opportunity to build the strong and scalable network with other members of the Association.
